Help

Use this dashboard to add new institute content, prepare uploaded files, and review what Owlbot can already use. This helps institute staff keep information current without editing code.

Good content to upload

  • Updated institute pages
  • Blog posts
  • Program descriptions
  • Contact information
  • FAQs
  • Public reports or resources

Do not upload

  • Private student information
  • Sensitive internal documents
  • Drafts that are not ready to share publicly
  • Anything the public should not see
